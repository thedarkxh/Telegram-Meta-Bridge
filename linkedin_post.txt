🚀 Excited to share a massive technical milestone for my media project, Teds Mordare Official!

Over the last few weeks, I’ve been building a fully automated News Broadcasting Engine from scratch. 

Managing news requires speed, and doing it manually just wasn't cutting it. So, I engineered a Python-based bridge that puts our entire Instagram distribution on autopilot. 🤖

Here is how the pipeline works:
1️⃣ Ingestion: The system monitors our central Telegram channel for breaking news updates in real-time.
2️⃣ Media Processing: It intercepts raw images and dynamically runs them through Pillow and FFmpeg to generate cinematic, 1080x1920 videos with blurred backgrounds, premium overlays, and embedded background music.
3️⃣ AI Captions: The text is parsed, cleaned, and injected with algorithm-friendly Call-To-Actions and SEO hashtags.
4️⃣ Distribution: Using the instagrapi library, the finished Reel is instantly published to our Instagram page.

No human intervention. Zero delay. ⚡

Building this taught me a ton about API rate-limit handling, persistent session management, and automated media rendering.
